❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my WhatsApp Status video blurry?

WhatsApp automatically destroys video quality to save bandwidth. StatusDrop pre-compresses your video to the exact specs WhatsApp expects — so it stays HD quality when posted as a Status.

How do I upload a long video to WhatsApp Status?

WhatsApp Status only allows 29-second videos. StatusDrop automatically splits your long video into multiple 29-second clips and sends them all directly to your WhatsApp — completely free!

Is StatusDrop really free?

Yes! 100% free. No login, no account, no watermark, no hidden fees. Upload videos up to 300MB and receive them in HD quality directly on WhatsApp.

What video formats are supported?

StatusDrop supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, 3GP and WMV formats up to 300MB in file size.

Is my video private and secure?

Yes! Your video is automatically deleted from our servers within 5 minutes after being sent to your WhatsApp. We never store or share your videos.
